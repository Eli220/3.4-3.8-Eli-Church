About Our School

Hutt Valley High School, Te Kura Tuarua o Te Awakairangi was founded in 1926. We focus on providing a high-quality education that ensures our students have the best opportunity to develop their strengths and talents and achieve success in a way that gives them a purposeful and rewarding future life.
